riecovest.random_matrices.sample_complex_gaussian

riecovest.random_matrices.sample_complex_gaussian(mean, cov, rng, num_samples)

Sample from a complex Gaussian distribution with given mean and covariance matrix.

Parameters:

  • mean (complex ndarray of shape (dim,)) – Mean of the complex Gaussian distribution.

  • cov (complex ndarray of shape (dim, dim)) – Covariance matrix of the complex Gaussian distribution.

  • rng (numpy.random.Generator) – Random number generator.

  • num_samples (int) – Number of samples to draw.

Returns:

sample – Complex Gaussian samples.

Return type:

ndarray of shape (dim, num_samples)
